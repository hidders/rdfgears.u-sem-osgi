Welcome to U-Sem!

U-Sem is a user modeling infrastructure for the Social Web that is developed by the WIS group at TU Delft in collaboration with the Knowledge Representation and Reasoning (KRR) Group at Leeds University. Funded and inspired by the ImREAL (FP7) research project, it provides means for creating user modeling services that support specific application needs.
